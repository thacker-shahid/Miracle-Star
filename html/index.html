About Us

A Talent Show is an event in which participants perform the arts of singing, dancing, acting, playing an instrument, poetry, comedy or other activities to showcase their skills. We are going to organize an event named “Miracle Star” in Bahraich. In which we give you an opportunity to show your talent publicly. The show will be judged by some reputed and experianced judges on the basis of your performance and there will be three winners in each domains (singing,dancing,mimicry etc.). The winners will be awarded with exciting cash Prizes and other certifications. The event will give you a perfect platform to show what Talent you have in you which makes you unique. This event will be on district level in which you can show your hidden person of yourself to the audience and get the chance to become the upcoming future star. You can step out of your comfort zone to experience the gift of life and to know your strength and weaknesses.
